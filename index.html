About Gems

Gems are colorful, button-shaped chocolates coated with a hard candy shell. Known for their bright colors and crunchy texture, Gems have been a favorite among candy lovers for generations.

Fun Facts

  • Gems come in a variety of colors, including blue, green, yellow, and pink.
  • The candy coating keeps the chocolate inside from melting, making them great for carrying on the go.
  • First launched in India, Gems has since become a popular treat worldwide.

Nutritional Information

A typical serving (about 20 pieces) contains approximately 150 calories, 6g of fat, and 18g of sugar. Perfect for a sweet treat!
